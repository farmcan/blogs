# Blog Templates

Use the smallest structure that matches the post. These templates are starting points, not rigid forms.

## 1. How-To

Best for tutorials, walkthroughs, setup guides, and process explanations.

```markdown
# <Title>

Intro:
- What problem this solves
- Who this is for
- What outcome the reader should expect

## Why This Matters

## What You Need Before You Start

## Step 1

## Step 2

## Common Pitfalls

## Next Steps
```

## 2. Announcement

Best for launches, releases, feature updates, and milestone posts.

```markdown
# <Title>

Intro:
- What changed
- Why now
- Who is affected

## What Changed

## Why It Matters

## What You Can Do Now

## What Happens Next
```

## 3. Thought Piece

Best for opinionated essays, strategy posts, and lessons learned.

```markdown
# <Title>

Intro:
- Tension or problem
- Point of view
- What the reader will take away

## The Situation

## What We Learned

## Where Most Teams Get Stuck

## A Better Default

## Closing Thought
```

## 4. Roundup

Best for curated links, research summaries, event recaps, or periodic digests.

```markdown
# <Title>

Intro:
- Theme of this roundup
- Why these items matter together

## Key Takeaways

## Item 1

## Item 2

## Item 3

## What To Watch Next
```

## Selection Rules

- Use `how-to` when the reader needs to do something.
- Use `announcement` when the news is the point.
- Use `thought-piece` when interpretation matters more than chronology.
- Use `roundup` when curation is the value.

## Frontmatter Baseline

```yaml
---
title: Example title
slug: example-title
date: 2026-03-16
author: OpenClaw Team
summary: One-sentence summary for feeds and previews.
tags:
  - example
draft: true
---
```
